How to Use

1
Upload Your Dataset
Upload the detailed Excel or CSV file you want to summarize.
2
Select Group By Columns
Choose the columns to group your data by (e.g., Region, Department, Product Category).
3
Choose Aggregations
Select which numeric columns to aggregate and which operations to apply: Sum, Count, Average, Min, Max.
4
Build Summary
Click Build Summary. Datacorex processes the entire dataset and generates the summary table.
5
Download Output
Download the clean summary Excel file — formatted and ready to use.
